== Key Features ==
###Send WhatsApp Messages Effortlessly
Say goodbye to the hassle of switching between platforms. With SyncMate, you can configure WhatsApp in just a few simple steps, all under 5 minutes. It's a one-time setup that saves you valuable time, allowing you to focus on achieving better outcomes and boosting productivity.

###WooCommerce Integration
Boost your online store's customer engagement by sending order updates, promotions, and product recommendations directly to your customers' WhatsApp. Keep your customers informed and engaged throughout their shopping journey.
Here are the features of WooCommerce integration::

- Pending Orders Notification:

Automatically notify customers via WhatsApp when their order status changes to pending.
Keep customers informed about the progress of their purchase journey right from the start.

- Processing Orders Notification:

Send real-time updates to customers on WhatsApp as their orders move through the processing stage.
Provide reassurance to customers that their orders are being handled promptly.

- Completed Orders Notification:

Celebrate completed orders by sending instant notifications to customers on WhatsApp.
Include a thank you message and encourage customers to provide feedback or make repeat purchases.

- On-Hold Orders Notification:

Notify customers on WhatsApp if their orders are placed on hold for any reason.
Provide explanations or instructions for resolving any issues with the order.

- Refunded Orders Notification:

Alert customers via WhatsApp when their orders are refunded, ensuring transparency and customer satisfaction.
Include details about the refund process and any additional information customers may need.

- Cancelled Orders Notification:

Send cancellation notifications to customers on WhatsApp if their orders are cancelled.
Offer alternative solutions or incentives to encourage customers to reconsider their purchase.

- Failed Orders Notification:

Inform customers promptly via WhatsApp if their orders fail to go through.
Provide assistance or guidance on how customers can proceed with their purchase successfully.

- Dynamic Invoice PDF Delivery:

Automatically generate dynamic invoice PDFs containing order details.
Send personalized invoice PDFs directly to customers on WhatsApp for their reference and records.

###Contact Form 7 Integration
Ensure prompt customer service by integrating Contact Form 7 with SyncMate. Receive customer inquiries from your website's contact form and respond instantly via WhatsApp. Streamline communication by sending responses directly to customers on WhatsApp, providing efficient and convenient support.

###Customized Message Templates
Easily craft personalized WhatsApp message templates that reflect your brand's identity. Customize your messages to fit various scenarios like order confirmations, shipping updates, and exclusive offers. With customizable templates, you can effortlessly maintain consistency and engage customers effectively across different interactions.

###Real-Time Analytics
Track the performance of your WhatsApp campaigns with real-time analytics. Monitor the delivery, open rates, and responses to improve your messaging strategy and customer engagement.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `syncmate-by-assistro` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the **Plugins** screen (**Plugins > Installed Plugins**).

You will find **SyncMate** menu in your WordPress admin screen.
